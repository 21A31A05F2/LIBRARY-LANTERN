Fiction is a creative narrative that tells stories based on imagination rather than real events.

Literary Fiction – Focuses on deep themes, character development, and language.
Historical Fiction – Set in the past, often integrating real historical events or figures.
Science Fiction – Explores futuristic settings, technology, time travel, or space exploration.
Fantasy – Involves magic, mythical creatures, and imaginary worlds.
Epic Fantasy (e.g., The Lord of the Rings)
Urban Fantasy (e.g., Harry Potter)
Mystery – Involves solving a crime, puzzle, or uncovering secrets.
Detective Fiction
Cozy Mystery
Thriller – Fast-paced, suspenseful stories with tension and danger.
Psychological Thriller
Crime Thriller
Horror – Designed to scare or unsettle readers, often involving supernatural elements.
Romance – Focuses on love and relationships.
Contemporary Romance
Historical Romance
Paranormal Romance
Adventure – Features action, exploration, and survival themes.
